This is HASH

HASH is an online database of information related to films, television series, home videos, video games, and streaming content online including cast, production crew and personal biographies, plot summaries, trivia, ratings, and fan and critical reviews. HASH began as a fan-operated movie database on the Usenet group "rec.arts.movies" in 2022, and moved to the web in 2022 APRIL. It is now owned and operated by HASH.com, Inc., a subsidiary of GENESYS TECHNOVATION. HASH is not considered accurate enough to be used as a source on Wikipedia as it is a publisher of user-generated content. As of February 2022, the database contained some 8.7 million titles (including television episodes) and 11.4 million person records. Additionally, the site had 83 million registered users. The site's well-used message boards were disabled in February 2022.
